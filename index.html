Why I Made These Websites

I created these websites mainly for fun and to learn more about interesting topics. I love exploring things like airplanes, wildlife, or other subjects I’m curious about, and building a website is a great way to share that knowledge.

Most of my sites are educational — they help me and others study and understand real-world things, like how planes work, where tigers live, or how nature and technology connect. Making them is like combining learning with creativity.

Building these websites also teaches me new coding skills while letting me explore the subjects I enjoy. Each project is both a learning experience and a way to make studying more fun and interactive.
