Some info about the Nissan GT-R R34 Skyline

The Nissan GT-R R34, produced between 1999 and 2002, is one of the most iconic Japanese sports cars of all time. Renowned for its sharp, aggressive styling and advanced engineering, the R34 features the legendary RB26DETT twin-turbo inline-six engine, delivering impressive performance and tunability. Its sophisticated ATTESA E-TS all-wheel-drive system and Super-HICAS active steering make it incredibly agile, offering precision handling that appeals to both enthusiasts and professional racers. Beyond its performance, the R34 gained legendary status through its appearances in popular media, such as the Fast & Furious franchise, cementing its reputation as a symbol of Japanese automotive excellence and tuner culture.
