This is a cat website — enjoy!

Cats are one of the most common pets in the world. They have whiskers that help them sense the air around them so they know what’s nearby. Today, you can find thousands of breeds, personalities, and coat patterns adored by people everywhere.

Sometimes when cats are grooming and you pet them, they might bite or grab you — not because they’re mean, but because they think you’re interrupting their routine or they misread your movement. Cats also sleep a lot—often 12–16 hours a day.

How cats are feisty

Cats can be little daredevils! They love to knock things over, scratch, and run around the house at lightning speed. Some cats love going outside—eating grass, rolling on the ground, roaming like tiny explorers.

Owning a cat is a lot of fun, but also a lot of work. You have to clean up after them, provide enrichment, trim claws or supply scratching posts, and keep litter boxes tidy. They reward you with companionship and quirky charm.

About This Site

This website was created by Pieter Tschopp as his very first website. Pieter has always loved cats and wanted a space to share fun facts, cute pictures, and stories.

While the site is simple, it is a stepping stone in Pieter’s journey into web development. He built it from scratch using HTML, CSS, and a small bit of JavaScript for animations and navigation highlighting.

Future plans include interactive features, mini‑games, and animations to make exploring cats even more exciting. For now, visitors can enjoy learning about feline behavior and personality.
