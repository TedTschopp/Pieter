This is a cat website — enjoy!

    

Cats are one of the most common pets in the world. They have whiskers that help them sense the air around them so they know what’s nearby. Today, you can find thousands of videos and pictures of cats online.

    

Sometimes when cats are grooming and you pet them, they might bite or grab you — not because they’re mean, but because they think you’re a predator. Cats also sleep a lot — sometimes up to 16 hours a day!

       
  
    

How cats are feisty

    

Cats are little daredevils! They love to knock things over, scratch, and run around the house at lightning speed. Some cats love going outside — eating grass, rolling on the ground, roaming the backyard, and chasing little critters.

    

Owning a cat is a lot of fun, but also a lot of work. You have to clean up after them, repair things they knock down, and of course, take care of their litter box. But if you can handle the chaos, you’ll have a loyal little friend.

  
  
    

About This Site

    

This website was created by Pieter Tschopp as his very first website. Pieter has always loved cats and wanted to make a space where he could share fun facts, cute pictures, and stories about cats with everyone.

    

While this site is simple, it is a stepping stone in Pieter’s journey into web development. He built it from scratch using HTML and CSS, carefully designing the layout, fonts, colors, and navigation to make it fun and easy to explore.

    

Future plans for the site include interactive features, mini-games, and animations to make exploring cats even more exciting. For now, visitors can enjoy learning about the fascinating behaviors, quirky habits, and adorable antics of cats from around the world.

    

Pieter hopes this website inspires others to appreciate the charm of cats and encourages beginners to start building their own websites. Every line of code, every color choice, and every cat fact was carefully considered to make this site a welcoming and playful experience.
